Description

Discover the magic of a toy workshop where your child gets to build toys and then try them out.

The game includes 10 different toys, each with their own minigame once the toy is assembled: Teddy bear, Whack-a-mole, Remote controlled car, Bubble making set, Xylophone, Boat, Easel and paints, Beach ball, Woodblocks, Yo-yo

Designed for preschool and kindergarten kids. Optimized for tablets. No reading required.
